What Entrepreneurship Means Here

Entrepreneurship is not just business creation. It is:

  • Problem-solving in the real world
  • Turning ideas into action
  • Learning how to build value for people
  • Developing discipline, clarity, and execution skills
  • Understanding failure as part of growth
